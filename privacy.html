Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

Introduction

TrainingExerciseLibrary.com ("we," "us," or "our") is operated by Schneider Saddlery. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our website at TrainingExerciseLibrary.com (the "Site"). By using the Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, as well as information collected automatically when you use the Site.

Information You Provide:

  • Email address and name when you sign up to save favorites or subscribe to our newsletter
  • Exercise submissions including your name, email, and exercise details when you submit a training exercise
  • Communications when you contact us directly

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Usage data such as pages viewed, time spent on pages, and exercises accessed
  • Device information including browser type, operating system, and screen resolution
  • Log data including IP address, access times, and referring URLs
  • Cookies and similar technologies for site functionality and analytics

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Training Exercise Library service
  • To enable the favorites feature and personalized exercise lists
  • To send you newsletter content and updates about new exercises (with your consent)
  • To review and publish submitted exercises
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the Site
  • To communicate with you about the Site, including responding to inquiries
  • To inform you about products and services from Schneider Saddlery that may interest you

Local Storage

The Site uses your browser's local storage to save your favorites list and preferences locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly export or share it. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Schneider Saddlery: As part of the Schneider Saddlery Equine Network, your information may be shared with Schneider Saddlery for marketing purposes related to equestrian products and services
  • Service providers: We may share information with third-party service providers who assist in operating the Site, such as email delivery services and analytics providers
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests

Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on the Site and store certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some features of the Site may not function properly without cookies.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your information
  • The right to opt out of marketing communications
  • The right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
